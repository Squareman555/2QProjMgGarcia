Who are the Toplaners?

Toplaners are the ones who are assigned to go to the middle of the map. Their primary job is to farm, and visit other lanes if necessary.

The champions who are played in this lane are usually tanks, and those who can melt down tanks. Some examples include: Aatrox (tank), Vayne (can melt tanks), among others.

the Top role is almost exactly the same as Mid, but on different locations on the map and different type of champions. To read about what the toplaner (or the midlaner) does on the Rift, please visit the Midlaner page.
